- cart-buttons-all-pages.js

What this update does:
1. Pay Now on fragrance pages goes to your checkout form first.
2. The checkout form still sends customer info to Formspree.
3. After Formspree accepts the order info, the customer is redirected to the matching Square checkout link for that exact fragrance and size.
4. The checkout page shows $6.00 shipping. Square should also add the $6.00 shipping because you set up the Square shipping profile.

Important:
- Automatic Square redirect is exact for one sample at a time.
- A cart with multiple different items cannot be sent to one exact Square link because Square payment links are single-item links.
