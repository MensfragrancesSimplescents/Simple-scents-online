HOW TO ADD THE VIEW CART BUTTON

1. Upload these two files to GitHub:
   cart.html
   view-cart-button.js

2. Open index.html.

3. At the very bottom, right before:
   </body>

4. Paste this:
   <script src="view-cart-button.js"></script>

5. Commit changes.

After that, the top-right Shop button on the homepage will become View Cart.
