HOW TO USE:
1. Upload size-selector-fixed.js to your GitHub website files.
2. Open every product/cologne HTML page.
3. Right before </body>, paste this line:

<script src="size-selector-fixed.js"></script>

This fixed version makes a big 1 mL / 2 mL choice box under the swipe photos text.
Default is 2 mL. Tapping 1 mL changes the visible price to the 1 mL price.
