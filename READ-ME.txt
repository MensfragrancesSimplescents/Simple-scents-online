Upload these 2 files to your website:

1. size-selector.css
2. size-selector.js

Then add these 2 lines to your HTML file:

Put this before </head>:
<link rel="stylesheet" href="size-selector.css">

Put this before </body>:
<script src="size-selector.js"></script>

What it does:
- Adds 1 mL and 2 mL buttons under the swipe left/right photo text.
- Uses 2 mL as the default.
- Updates the shown price when someone taps 1 mL or 2 mL.
- Saves the chosen size/price so your cart code can use it.
